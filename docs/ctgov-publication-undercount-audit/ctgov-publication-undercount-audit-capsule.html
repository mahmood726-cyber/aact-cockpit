    Glossary
    
    
      
    no-resultsthe study has never posted a results record on ClinicalTrials.gov.
    
      
    ghost protocolno results and no disposition record — a structurally silent registration. Closely tracks no-results because disposition is rarely filled in.
    
      
    result linka sponsor-submitted publication reference attached to the registry record.
    
      
    derived linka PubMed paper automatically indexed to the trial's NCT identifier — an external paper trail.
    
      
    ACT / FDAAA 801an "applicable clinical trial" of an FDA-regulated product, legally required to report results under the 2007 FDA Amendments Act; here approximated by FDA-regulated status.
